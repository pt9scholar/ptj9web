Motivation

Why commodities?

Commodities sit at the intersection of real assets, global trade, weather, finance, geopolitics, and everyday life. My academic work in applied economics and my experience in impact investing and supply chain operations have shown me how market movements affect both businesses and communities.

I am motivated to enter the commodity industry because it combines analytical rigor with tangible global impact. I want to help understand and manage risks — from FX volatility and price swings to logistics and sourcing challenges — while building markets that are more resilient, transparent, and connected.
