Motivation

Why commodities?

I am motivated to enter the commodity industry because commodities sit at the center of real economic activity. They connect farmers, producers, logistics networks, financial markets, and end consumers. My experience in Central America, supply chain operations, and impact investing has shown me how price volatility, FX risk, financing, and trade flows affect businesses on the ground.

I want to build a career where I can combine applied economics, data-driven analysis, and global market awareness to understand risk, support better decision-making, and contribute to markets that are essential to food systems, energy, infrastructure, and development.
